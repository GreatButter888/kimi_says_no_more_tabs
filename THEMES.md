# 🏎️ Kimi Räikkönen F1 Team Themes

The extension now features 5 beautiful themes based on each F1 team Kimi drove for during his legendary career!

## Available Themes

### 1. 🏎️ Ice Man (Ferrari) - **Default**
**Colors**: Ferrari Red (#DC0000) to Dark Red (#B30000)
- Kimi's most iconic team
- 2007 World Championship winner
- Ferrari stints: 2007-2009, 2014-2018
- 10 years total with the Scuderia

### 2. 🏁 McLaren Silver Arrow
**Colors**: Papaya Orange (#FF8700) to Light Blue (#47C7FC)
- Where Kimi became a superstar
- McLaren: 2002-2006
- 9 wins with the Silver Arrows
- Remembered for his incredible speed

### 3. 🏆 Lotus Black & Gold
**Colors**: Gold (#B8860B) to Black (#0D0D0D)
- Kimi's comeback team
- Lotus: 2012-2013
- 2 wins and proved he still had it
- Classic black and gold livery

### 4. 💙 Sauber Petronas
**Colors**: Petronas Blue (#0066B3) to Dark Blue (#0A1929)
- Where it all began
- Sauber: 2001 (Rookie season)
- Kimi's F1 debut
- Showed immediate promise

### 5. 🔴 Alfa Romeo Racing
**Colors**: Burgundy Red (#9B0000) to Dark Gray (#1C1C1C)
- Kimi's final team
- Alfa Romeo: 2019-2021
- Where the legend ended
- 349 race starts total

## How to Use

1. Click the ⚙️ settings icon in the top right of any new tab
2. Select your favorite F1 team theme from the dropdown
3. Click "Save Settings"
4. Your theme will persist across all new tabs!

## Theme Details

Each theme features:
- Beautiful gradient backgrounds matching team colors
- Authentic F1 team color schemes
- Smooth transitions when switching
- Persistent storage (remembers your choice)
- Visual preview boxes in settings

## Legacy Support

The extension maintains backward compatibility with the original themes:
- `ice` → Maps to Ice Man (Ferrari)
- `ferrari` → Maps to Ice Man (Ferrari)
- `f1` → Maps to Black & White classic theme
